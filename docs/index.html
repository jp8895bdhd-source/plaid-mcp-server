Privacy Policy

Personal Finance Dashboard
Operator: Alex Setzler

Overview

This privacy policy describes how the Personal Finance Dashboard ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information. This App is designed for personal use by a single user only.

Data Collection

The App collects the following financial data to function:

  • Bank account balances and account details
  • Transaction history
  • Liabilities (loans, credit card balances)

This data is collected through Plaid Inc., a third-party financial data aggregator that securely connects to your financial institutions.

Data Storage

All collected data is stored locally on your personal computer only. No data is transmitted to remote servers or cloud storage services beyond what is necessary for the initial connection through Plaid.

Third Parties

Plaid Inc. is the only third party that handles your financial data. Plaid is used solely to securely connect your financial accounts and retrieve your data. We do not share your information with any other third parties.

For details on how Plaid handles your data, see Plaid's privacy policy.

What We Don't Do

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No cookies or persistent identifiers
  • No data sharing with advertisers, marketers, or other services
  • No remote monitoring or telemetry
  • No selling or licensing of your data

Data Security

Your financial data is protected by local file encryption on your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your computer and the credentials used to access your financial accounts.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
